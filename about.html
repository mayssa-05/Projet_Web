About Study Group Matcher

Study Group Matcher is a web platform designed to help students connect for academic collaboration. Features include:

  • Posting requests for help or offers to assist.
  • Browsing posts to find suitable study partners.
  • A clean and responsive frontend using Bootstrap.
  • No real-time chat features, keeping it simple and easy to use.
